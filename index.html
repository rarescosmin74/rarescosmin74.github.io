Ask us anything!

Can we play with animals?

For sure! Out farm animals are always ready to meet new people and play with them. As long as you do not harm or disturb them, you are allowed to see, pet and even play with animals.

Where do you get your ingredients from?

90% of our ingredients come from our farm,  but some recipes require ingredients that can hardly grow in our crops. Ask the waiter if you want to consume something 100% local. Also, our gastronomy menu is 100% vegetarian or vegan with ingredients from our animals, procured without harming them.

Is it hygienic to eat this close to animals?


We can assure you that there is no problem with our animals. We have received authorization to function. Moreover, all of our animals are fully vaccinated and well cared.

Do you offer a parking lot?

Yes! We have a parking lot suitable for 15 cars.
